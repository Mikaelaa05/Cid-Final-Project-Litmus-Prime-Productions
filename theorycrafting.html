Diluc

Pyro DPS Build

Diluc is a powerful Pyro DPS character. His abilities can be used to deal massive AoE damage. Use the Crimson Witch of Flames set for increased Pyro damage and Vaporize/Melt reactions.

Physical DPS Build

While not as common, Diluc can be built as a physical DPS. Use Gladiator's Finale for increased physical damage and aim for critical hits.

Venti

Elemental Burst Support

Venti excels in crowd control and energy regeneration. Use the Viridescent Venerer set to boost Anemo damage and reduce enemy resistance to Swirl reactions.

Sub-DPS Build

For a sub-DPS build, focus on increasing Venti's Elemental Mastery to maximize the damage from Swirl reactions.

Ganyu

Freeze Team Composition

Ganyu shines in a Freeze team composition. Use the Blizzard Strayer set to increase Cryo damage and critical rate against frozen enemies.

Melt Team Composition

In a Melt team, Ganyu can deal massive damage by combining her Cryo abilities with Pyro characters. Focus on high attack and critical damage stats.

Zhongli

Shield Support Build

Zhongli's shields are some of the strongest in the game. Use the Tenacity of the Millelith set to increase shield strength and provide buffs to your team.

Geo DPS Build

While primarily a support, Zhongli can also be built as a Geo DPS. Use the Archaic Petra set to boost Geo damage and provide elemental damage bonuses to your team.

Fischl

Electro Support Build

Fischl excels as an Electro support character. Use the Thundering Fury set to increase Electro damage and the effectiveness of her Elemental Skill and Burst.

Physical DPS Build

Fischl can also be built as a physical DPS. Use the Bloodstained Chivalry and Pale Flame sets to maximize physical damage and increase attack speed.
