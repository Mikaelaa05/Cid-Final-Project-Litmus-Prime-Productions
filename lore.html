Mondstadt

The City of Freedom

Mondstadt, also known as the City of Freedom, is a place where the people are free to pursue their desires. Ruled by the Anemo Archon, Barbatos, Mondstadt is known for its picturesque landscapes and festive atmosphere.

Liyue

The Harbor of Contracts

Liyue Harbor, the City of Contracts, is a bustling commercial port where the God of Contracts, Morax, watches over. Known for its rich culture and historical traditions, Liyue is a land of prosperity and commerce.

Inazuma

The Nation of Eternity

Inazuma, under the rule of the Electro Archon, Raiden Shogun, seeks to achieve eternity. This isolated nation is characterized by its strict laws and powerful military, reflecting its pursuit of unchanging perfection.

Sumeru

The City of Wisdom

Sumeru is known as the City of Wisdom, where scholars and knowledge seekers gather. Governed by the Dendro Archon, Sumeru places a high value on knowledge and enlightenment.

Fontaine

The Nation of Justice

Fontaine is a place where justice and law prevail. The Hydro Archon oversees this nation, ensuring that fairness and order are maintained throughout the land.

This section delves into the intricate lore of Genshin Impact, exploring the unique stories and cultural aspects of each region within the game.
